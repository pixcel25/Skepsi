AI companies cannot retain users only based on giving the best model since the best model is a moving target so only the experience of the app actually matters since even if the model is slightly weaker but the experience is good the user is more likely to stay  for the experience itself.

MCP's,memory,response style all this matters a similar example is browsers. Most browsers are almost similar they have very few differentiating features if  you pay attention. People stay on Chrome not because it is the best but the convenience. Same for AI models  I am predicting more AI companies will stick to locking the user to the app itself rather than the model so even if the model degrades slightly the convenience makes them stay. A user is less likely to leave if my AI remembers my habits gives me my responses exactly as I want, connects to my apps exactly as I want to even If I don't have the best model. I am so used to my current AI for my workflow I will  be less likely to leave.
